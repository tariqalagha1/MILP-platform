# MILP Platform — User Guide
### What It Is, How It Works, and What You Can Expect

## WHAT IS MILP?

Imagine you run a clinic — or a chain of clinics.

Every month, you submit insurance claims, see patients, book appointments, and manage staff.
But at the end of the month, the revenue number is always lower than it should be. You know
something is leaking. You just don't know where, how much, or what to do about it.

**MILP fixes that.**

MILP is a dashboard you open on your computer or phone that shows you — in Saudi Riyals —
exactly how much money your clinic is losing, why it is happening, and what to do to get it
back.

That's it. No complicated setup. No training courses. Just a clear picture of your revenue,
your risks, and your recovery plan.

---

## THE THREE PROBLEMS MILP SOLVES

### Problem 1 — Insurance Claims Getting Rejected

When you submit a claim to an insurance company and it gets rejected, that money is lost
unless someone in your team catches it, fixes it, and resubmits it in time.

Most clinics lose 10–15% of their claims revenue this way — not because the work wasn't done,
but because the paperwork had a small error that nobody caught in time.

**What MILP does:** Before a claim is even rejected, MILP gives it a risk score. A red badge
means this claim is likely to get rejected — fix it now. A green badge means it looks clean.
If a claim does get rejected, MILP shows you exactly which field was wrong and gives you a
one-click button to send it back to be fixed.

---

### Problem 2 — Patients Who Don't Show Up

A patient books an appointment. They don't show up. That slot is empty. That revenue is gone.

The GCC average is 8.5% of all appointments ending as no-shows. For a clinic doing 200
appointments a month, that's 17 empty slots every single month.

**What MILP does:** MILP looks at each patient's history and calculates their no-show risk.
High-risk patients automatically get reminder messages sent to them before their appointment
— reducing no-shows and filling your schedule.

---

### Problem 3 — Patients Who Disappear

A patient visits once, gets treated, and never comes back. Not because they don't need
follow-up care — but because nobody called them.

**What MILP does:** MILP tracks which patients are due for follow-up and haven't been
contacted. It flags them so your team can reach out — turning a one-time visit into an
ongoing patient relationship.

## HOW MILP WORKS — STEP BY STEP

### Step 1 — You Enter Your Numbers

When you first sign up, MILP asks you five simple questions:

1. How many appointments do you have per month?
2. What is the average value of an appointment in SAR?
3. What percentage of patients don't show up?
4. What percentage of your insurance claims get rejected?
5. What percentage of patients come back for repeat visits?

You don't need to dig through spreadsheets. Rough estimates work fine for a first calculation.

---

### Step 2 — MILP Calculates Your Leakage

Within seconds, MILP shows you:

- **How much money you are losing per month** — broken down by no-shows, rejected claims,
  and missed follow-ups
- **How much of that is recoverable** — in the next 90 days specifically
- **What your annual recovery potential is** if you fix these issues

For example, a Growth-tier clinic chain generating SAR 300,000 per month typically discovers
SAR 35,000–55,000 in recoverable revenue they didn't know was sitting there.

---

### Step 3 — You See Your Live Dashboard

Once your clinic is connected, MILP shows you a live dashboard with:

**Your revenue today.** Total appointments completed, total claims submitted, total value
in the pipeline.

**Your risk alerts.** Which claims are at risk of rejection. Which patients are likely to
no-show. Which patients are overdue for follow-up.

**Your recovery actions.** Not just alerts — actual buttons to take action. Fix this claim.
Send this reminder. Call this patient.

---

### Step 4 — Your Team Acts on the Alerts

MILP is built for the people who work in your clinic — not just the owner.

Your billing team sees the claims dashboard. They know which claims need attention today.

Your front desk team sees the appointment dashboard. They know which patients need a reminder
call.

Your clinic manager sees the branch dashboard. They know which locations are performing and
which ones need attention.

Each person sees what they need to do. Nothing more, nothing less.

---

### Step 5 — You Review the Results

At the end of the month, MILP shows you a report you can share with your board, your
investors, or your management team.

It shows:

- Revenue recovered from rejected claims
- No-show rate improvement
- Patient follow-up conversion rate
- SAR impact of each improvement

These are not estimates. These are actual numbers from your clinic.

## PRIVACY AND DATA SECURITY

Your patients' clinical data is never stored in MILP.

MILP works with financial and operational data only:

- Appointment volumes, values, and statuses
- Claim amounts, statuses, and rejection reasons
- Anonymous patient risk scores (no names, no medical records)

All data is encrypted. Access is controlled by role — your billing team cannot see branch
financial data unless you give them permission. Your front desk cannot see insurance claim
details unless their role includes it.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Do I need to replace my current clinic management system?**
No. MILP works alongside your existing system — Nphies, iCare, or any other. It reads your
data and adds the intelligence layer on top. You keep using what you already have.

**Is it difficult to learn?**
No. If you can read a WhatsApp message, you can use MILP. The dashboard is designed for
clinic operators, not software engineers.

**What if my team doesn't use technology?**
MILP is designed for non-technical users. The most important actions — fix this claim, call
this patient — are a single button tap. No training course required.

**How quickly will I see ROI?**
Most clinics see measurable revenue recovery within the first 30 days. Some within the first
week, as the billing team catches and corrects high-risk claims before submission deadlines.

**Is my data safe?**
Yes. MILP uses the same security infrastructure as major international banks — encrypted
storage, role-based access, and no storage of patient clinical records.

**What does it cost?**
Pricing starts at SAR 4,000 per month for a single clinic. For a clinic recovering SAR 20,000+
per month in lost revenue, the subscription pays for itself within the first week.

**What languages does MILP support?**
Currently English with Arabic localization in development. All currency is displayed in SAR.
